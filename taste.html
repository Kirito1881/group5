The senses of smell and taste tend to work together. If one could not smell something, they could not taste it either. The sense of taste is also known as gustaoception. Taste buds on the tongue contain chemoreceptors that work similarly to the chemoreceptors in the nasal cavity. However, the chemoreceptors in the nose would detect any kind of smell, whereas there are four different types of taste buds and each one can detect different types of tastes like sweetness, sourness, bitterness and saltiness.
